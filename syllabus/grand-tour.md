# The Grand Tour

All 196 texts in the Enchiridion library, organized chronologically by era and grouped by subject within each era. This is the complete curriculum for someone interested in reading everything over a long period of time.

**How to use this syllabus:** The general recommendation is to proceed chronologically, era by era. Within each era, texts are grouped by subject — but the grouping is for navigation, not prescription. Read broadly across subjects, follow your curiosity, and dive deep where interest takes you. A "some of all, all of some" approach works well: sample widely, then commit fully to what compels you.
